# Workspace settings

The **Settings** section of a workspace is where you configure the workspace itself and
publish your apps. Open it from the gear icon in the workspace navigation.

## What you can configure

- **General information** — the workspace name and basic details.
- **Publishing** — deploy your apps and manage their live status (see
  [Publishing](../apps/publishing.mdx)).
- **Connections** — manage the [SQL databases](../data/connecting-sql.mdx) and
  [APIs](../apis/overview.mdx) connected to the workspace.
- **People** — invite and manage [users](../access-control/users.mdx) and their roles.

## Backups

SuperToolMake stores its data in the internal database. When self-hosting, make sure to
back up the data volumes described in [Installation](../getting-started/installation.mdx)
before upgrading. The settings area surfaces backup status so you can spot problems.

## Instance administration

Instance admins (the first [admin user](../getting-started/workspace.mdx#creating-an-admin-user)
and anyone they promote) can manage settings that apply across the whole instance, in
addition to workspace settings.
