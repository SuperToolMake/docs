# Publishing

Apps aren't visible to end users until they're **published**. Publishing deploys your
latest changes and makes the live version available.

## Publishing from the builder

Use the **Publish** button in the builder to deploy your workspace. You can also toggle
an individual app **Live** (and **Stop** it again) directly from the builder toolbar.

| Control | Result |
| --- | --- |
| **Publish** | Deploys your changes so they're available to end users. |
| **Live** toggle | Makes the app available, or takes it offline. |
| **View live app** | Opens the published version in a new tab. |

## Preview vs live

- **Preview** shows your work-in-progress as an end user would experience it, without
  publishing. Use it to test before going live.
- **Live** is the published version your end users actually access.

Changes you make in the builder don't reach end users until you publish again.

## Who can see a published app

Once live, an app is accessible according to its [roles](../access-control/roles.mdx).
End users only see the screens and data their role permits, and only the apps they've
been granted access to appear in their portal.

## Sharing the app

Share the app's live URL with your end users, or have them sign in to the portal where
the apps they can access are listed. Manage who has access from the
[Users](../access-control/users.mdx) section.
